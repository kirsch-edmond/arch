These Are Some Tips I Shared With My Clients To Help
Them Get The Building plan Of their Choice .

You see if you want to build a modern house , you really have to know your budget . I'll assume you have a site plan and indenture for your land . If you don't you have to get a qualified surveyor to do that for you .

Now , back to the budget , do not even attempt to start a building you know you can't finish . It makes the area look bad, It is a souce of regret , and overall it leaves you very dissatified .

Second thing is know what you want . Let me take you back to school . Remember the 5 W's . Who , What, Which, Where and Why . Let me introduce you to the 5 W's of building . This is a concept I've made through my experience .

  • Who : who are you building it for ? Is it for your mother ? Father ? Yourself ?
  • What : What are you building ? A mansion ? A villa ?
  • Which : which type of design is it Modern ? Classical ?
  • Where: Where is it to be built ? On a cliffside ? In the city ?
  • Why : why are you building it ? For fame ? For your family ? For status?

And lastly , does it make you happy ? Do you look at the design and smile ?
